### Greater Ferrous Burst
#### V1: Base

Home Rules: None

Notation: Mt-Ml-Ae-Ci-x0x-Dm-xt
Formula: [[Spells/Pure Types/Metal/Metal\|Metal]]-Enhancement-Area-Circle-10-Damage-2
